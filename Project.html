Our Client

AngelHaus Inc. is a housing organization in Greater Victoria that is currently looking to accommodate student needs for housing with a SaaS product. They are a very altruistic company that aims to ease the burden of finding a house and living in Victoria for students. Sunny Raccoons has been approached to implement a software solution. It will be our job to deliver the best work on our client’s wishes.

Our Executive Summary

AngelHaus is a mobile application that aims to help renters, especially students, find suitable housing. The application aims to centralize housing and renting information in B.C by gathering and compiling references and ratings of each tenant or landlord. The platform will provide rigorous I.D. and background checks for renters and tenants, implementing a third party government body that oversees the interactions between both parties. AngelHaus oversees and mediates interactions to prevent either party from abusing contracts or contributing to any other inappropriate communications.
